Dungeon Meshi

Ah, Dungeon Meshi. A show about cooking the monsters in a dungeon. Funny and silly until it decides to be a little more sinister. So many things are still shrouded in mystery, and each character is a delight to watch interact with each other(my favorite is Senshi hes so silly billy). The dungeon feels like a real ecosystem, immaculate world building. It has 24 episodes, all of which satiate my hunger.
